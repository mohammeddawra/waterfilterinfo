Lead & Heavy Metals

Corroding pipes can leach lead into your water, causing developmental issues in children and cardiovascular problems in adults. Even safe levels accumulate over time.

PFAS "Forever Chemicals"

These toxic compounds don't break down in nature or your body. Linked to cancer, thyroid disease, and weakened immune systems. Found in 99% of Americans blood.

🦠

Bacteria & Viruses

Outdated infrastructure and treatment failures can introduce harmful microorganisms. E. coli, Legionella, and other pathogens cause 7.15 million illnesses yearly.

💊

Pharmaceutical Residue

Traces of antibiotics, hormones, and painkillers pass through water treatment. These endocrine disruptors affect fertility, metabolism, and child development.

🧴

Chlorine & Disinfectants

While killing bacteria, chlorine creates harmful byproducts linked to bladder cancer and miscarriages. That pool smell? It is a chemical reaction you should not drink.
